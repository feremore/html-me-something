New Study Finds That Pigs Do In Fact Fly!

Everyone has heard the old phrase "I'll believe that when pigs fly.", however the truth has been a well kept secret until now.

I was in disbelief when I found out but the truth will startle you to your core. Pig farmers are planning to take over the world with their vast pig army and want us to be none the wiser to their aerial superiority. This plan has been set in motion hundreds of years ago by the oppressed early livestock farmers in order to get back at their overlords. They spent centuries training the pigs in airspace combat in the dark of night so people wouldn't catch on to their plans. However, due to increase in demand of pork they started making money off of their swine armies instead of utilizing them for their original combat purposes.

But the real question is why don't they use their abilities to fly away from their current captivity? Well the truth is pigs are only able to fly if they hear the correct command phrase in pig latin. It is only known to the pig farmer elites that bide their time till it becomes necessary to protect their intrest again.
